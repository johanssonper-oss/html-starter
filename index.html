The Course

Welcome to Vadrózsa, a premiere 9-hole golf destination nestled within the rolling hills of the Balaton region. Our course is designed to harmonize with the natural Hungarian landscape, offering both challenge and serenity.

Our course offers you the chance to play and enjoy the wonderful scenery of the Balaton Hills. You can come alone and pair up with other players, play on your own or bring a bigger group of friends and arrange a full day with the course all by yourselves. We can accomodate any food, beverage or other program requirements to arrange a perfect day for you all. Our Goal: To provide a sophisticated and personalized environment where every detail is managed so you can focus entirely on your game and the scenery.
