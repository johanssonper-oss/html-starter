The Course

Welcome to Vadrózsa, a premier 9-hole golf destination nestled within the rolling hills of the Balaton region. Our course is designed to harmonize with the natural Hungarian landscape, offering both challenge and serenity.

The Experience: Play solo, pair up with local members, or reserve the entire course for a fully customized corporate or private group day.
Tailored Amenities: From premium equipment rentals to bespoke food and beverage catering, we accommodate every detail of your perfect day.
Our Goal: To provide a sophisticated, personalized environment where you can focus entirely on your game and the breathtaking landscape.
